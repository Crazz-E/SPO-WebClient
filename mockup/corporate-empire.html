Design Principles
📈
Data First
Monospace numbers with tabular alignment. Sparklines & trend arrows inline. Every value scannable in under 2 seconds.
🌐
Neutral Dark
No green tint. Pure neutral backgrounds let data colors (green/red/gold) carry their full semantic weight.
👑
Gold = Your Empire
Gold accent reserved for identity, wealth, rank, and active selections. Everything "yours" glows gold.
💻
Solid Panels
Glass only on InfoWidget & minimap. All data panels are solid and opaque for maximum readability.
